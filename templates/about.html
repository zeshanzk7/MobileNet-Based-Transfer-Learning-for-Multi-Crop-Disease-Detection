About CropCare AI

Our Mission

CropCare AI is designed to help farmers and agricultural experts quickly identify crop diseases using artificial intelligence. Our goal is to reduce crop losses and improve yield through early disease detection and expert recommendations.

Technology

Our system uses deep learning with MobileNetV2 architecture, trained on the comprehensive PlantVillage dataset containing over 87,000 images of healthy and diseased crops. The model achieves high accuracy in detecting 38+ disease classes across multiple crops.

Supported Crops

  • Tomato - 10 disease classes
  • Potato - 3 disease classes
  • Apple - 4 disease classes
  • Grape - 4 disease classes
  • Corn (Maize) - 4 disease classes
  • Pepper (Bell) - 2 disease classes
  • Cherry, Peach, Strawberry, and more

How It Works

  1. Upload: Take a clear photo of the affected crop leaf
  2. Analyze: Our AI model processes the image in seconds
  3. Results: Get disease identification with confidence score
  4. Action: Follow recommended prevention and treatment measures

Benefits

  • Early disease detection
  • Reduce crop losses
  • Expert recommendations
  • Quick and accurate results
  • Accessible to all farmers
  • No expert required on-site

Dataset

Our model is trained on the PlantVillage Dataset, one of the largest public datasets for plant disease detection. The dataset includes:

  • 87,000+ high-quality images
  • 38 disease classes
  • 14 crop species
  • Images captured under controlled conditions
